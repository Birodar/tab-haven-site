Questions

Where are my tabs stored?

Only in your own browser. Tab Haven has no servers and never uploads your tabs or your browsing.

Do I need an account?

No. Install it and start saving tabs right away. Nothing to sign up for.

Can I move to a new computer?

Yes. Use Export backup to save a file, then Import it on the other computer. Your Pro license works on up to 5 devices with the license key from your purchase email.

How does the free plan compare to others?

The free plan already does more than most paid tools: unlimited saving, search, import and manual backup. Pro adds unlimited colored folders, workspace switch, group and pinned tab preservation, notes, and automatic backups.

What is your refund policy?

Refunds are handled securely through Lemon Squeezy, our payment provider, under their standard policy. If Pro is not right for you, just reach out and we will take care of it.
