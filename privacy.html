Privacy Policy

Tab Haven by LTech (Lyubenov Technologies)

Tab Haven is built to be completely private. It runs entirely inside your own browser. We do not have servers, we do not run any analytics inside the extension, and we never collect, transmit, sell or share your data.

What Tab Haven stores

The tab sets you save, your folders, notes and backup snapshots are stored locally on your own device using your browser's built-in storage. This information never leaves your computer and is only accessible to you.

What Tab Haven does not do

  • No data collection of any kind.
  • No servers and no accounts. Nothing is uploaded.
  • No tracking, no advertising, no third-party analytics.
  • Your browsing history and open tabs are never sent anywhere.

Permissions we request, and why

  • Tabs and Tab groups: to read the tabs in your window when you save a set, and to reopen them (with their groups and pinned state) when you restore.
  • Storage and Unlimited storage: to keep your saved sets, folders, notes and local backups on your device.

These permissions are used only to make the extension work on your device. They are never used to collect or send data.

Payments

If you buy Tab Haven Pro, the payment is handled securely by our payment provider, Lemon Squeezy, who acts as the seller of record. Tab Haven itself does not see or store your payment details.

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.
